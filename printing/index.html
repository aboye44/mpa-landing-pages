Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

PDF (preferred), Adobe Illustrator (.ai), InDesign (.indd), Photoshop (.psd), and Microsoft Word. We provide free file review before production.

What is your typical turnaround time?

2-3 business days for most digital print jobs. Same-day and next-day rush available. Large offset runs may take 5-7 days.

Do you offer free proofs?

Yes. Every job gets a digital proof for approval before production. Press proofs available for color-critical work.

What's your minimum order?

No minimums for most digital printing. We print as few as 25 business cards or as many as 250,000 brochures.

Can you match specific colors (Pantone/PMS)?

Yes. Our Xerox Iridesse presses support Pantone matching, and we offer spot color on offset for exact brand color fidelity.

Do you offer design services?

Yes. Our design team can create your layout from scratch or refine your existing files. Design fees vary by complexity.

Can you ship nationwide?

Yes. We ship via UPS and USPS anywhere in the US. Local pickup and delivery available in the Lakeland/Tampa/Orlando area.
