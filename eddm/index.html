FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EDDM?

Every Door Direct Mail lets you send postcards to every residential address on specific postal routes without needing a mailing list. USPS delivers to every door.

What does “all-in” pricing include?

Print, postage, USPS preparation (bundling, facing slips, paperwork), and delivery to the Post Office. The only thing not included is design if you need it.

What size postcards work best for EDDM?

6.5″ × 9″ and 8.5″ × 11″ are the most popular. Larger pieces get more attention but cost more in postage. We’ll recommend the best size for your budget.

What’s the minimum order?

EDDM routes typically have 200–500 addresses each. Most campaigns start at 2,500 pieces (5–12 routes). No maximum.

Can you help with postcard design?

Yes. Our design team can create your postcard or work from your existing files. Design assistance is available for an additional fee.

How do I pick the right neighborhoods?

Use our free EDDM route planner to select carrier routes based on demographics, home value, and proximity to your business.
