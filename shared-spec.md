## Landing Page Architecture (ALL pages follow this)

### Navigation (STRIPPED — PPC only)
- Logo (left) — links to mailpro.org  
- Phone number (right) — click-to-call
- One CTA button (right) — "Get a Free Quote" or service-specific

### Hero Section
- Bold, keyword-matched headline (H1)
- Benefit-driven subheadline (2-3 sentences max)
- TWO CTAs: Primary button + Secondary "Call (863) XXX-XXXX"
- Trust bar immediately below: 3-4 credential badges
- Testimonial snippet (one strong quote with name/title)

### Embedded Lead Capture Form
- Multi-step or sidebar form — ALWAYS on page, not linked away
- Fields: Name, Company, Email, Phone, Service Type (dropdown), Brief Description
- Hidden GCLID field for offline conversion tracking
- Submit button: High-contrast red (#ED372A)
- Form headline: "Get Your Free Quote in 24 Hours"

### Social Proof Section
- 3-5 testimonials with names, companies, and specific outcomes
- Client logo bar (if available)
- Google review rating badge

### How It Works (3 steps)
- Simple numbered process: Request → Quote → Execute
- Icons for each step
- Reduces perceived complexity

### Comparison Table (MPA vs. Competition)
- Side-by-side with green checks for MPA, red X for typical competitor
- Specific to each service line

### FAQ Section
- 5-7 questions specific to the service
- Accordion style
- Addresses objections and includes pricing ranges where appropriate

### Final CTA Section
- Repeats the form or a prominent CTA button
- Urgency element: "Same-week turnaround available"

### Footer (minimal)
- MPA logo + tagline
- Phone + email (quotes@mailpro.org)
- Address: Lakeland, FL
- "Created with Perplexity Computer" link
- Copyright 2026

## PPC-Specific Requirements
1. NO full navigation — stripped header only
2. NO blog links, no service dropdowns, no exit paths
3. EVERY page has embedded form (not linked away)
4. GCLID capture via hidden form field
5. CallRail-ready phone numbers (dynamic number insertion compatible)
6. UTM parameter pass-through on form submissions
7. Page load target: < 2 seconds
8. Mobile-first design (60%+ traffic will be mobile)
9. Click-to-call prominent on mobile
10. Schema.org LocalBusiness markup on every page
